How to install Metamask and import SuNTOkEN on PancakeSwap (SNTK)

To buy some SuNTOkEN (SNTK) you first need to add the MetaMask plugin to Google Chrome or Firefox then create a Wallet. After that, "import the token". It is found at the bottom of metamask and add the custom token with this contract address:

(0x9eC1E983d89A8121D912e3489867842dba3d156e)
(SNTK) SuNTOkEN should be imported. You can also buy (SNTK) SuNTOkEN on PancakeSwap. Go on (https://pancakeswap.finance/), create or add a wallet if you haven't done so. Go on the Trade page. click on the swap box, go to the bottom where it says manage tokens. Avoid the "List" part and click on "Tokens" and add this contract address
(0x9eC1E983d89A8121D912e3489867842dba3d156e)
Now you can Buy, Trade and Sale (SNTK) SuNTOkEN not just on PancakeSwap but on the following exchanges as well. The same import technic that was done on PancakeSwap applies to the following platforms as well: Mdex, BSCswap, Anyswap, BSCStation Swap, CheeseSwap, O3swap, Bscex, Autofarm, ApeSwap, BakerySwap, Biswap, 1inch Network, ParaSwap. Alway make sure you are connected to some sort of a wallet like (Metamask)

Our features

Notable difference between tokens and coins is what they represent. While crypto coins are essentially digital versions of money, tokens can stand for assets or deeds. You can buy tokens with coins, but some tokens can carry more value than any of them.

What Are Metaverse Tokens? Metaverse tokens are a unit of virtual currency used to make digital transactions within the metaverse. Since metaverses are built on the blockchain, transactions on underlying networks are near-instant. Blockchains are designed to ensure trust and security, making the metaverse the perfect environment for an economy free of corruption and financial fraud. Holders of metaverse tokens can access multiple services and applications inside the virtual space. Some tokens give special in-game abilities. Other tokens represent unique items, like clothing for virtual avatars or membership for a community.

What is Payment Tokens - payment tokens have no other purpose than to pay for goods and services

What is an Equity Tokens - if a token represents some stock or equity in the company that issues it, it’s an equity token. However, few companies have attempted such an ICO because there isn’t much regulatory guidance about what is legal and what is not.

What is Utility Tokens - also called application tokens. They are used to provide people with access to either a product or service. They are also rare because most tokens are expected to gain in value based on their limited supply.
